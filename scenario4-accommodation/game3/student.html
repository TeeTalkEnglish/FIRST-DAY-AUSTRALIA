💬 Useful Phrases for Asking Questions

Starting a question
"Can I ask about...?" "I have a question about..." "What should I do about...?" "Is there a rule for...?"
Asking for more detail
"Sorry, what time exactly?" "How often?" "Which day?" "Can you say that again?"
Checking you understood
"So I need to...?" "Is that every week?" "Did you say...?" "Just to check..."
