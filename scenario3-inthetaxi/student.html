🚖 In the Taxi

Read the situation, then respond out loud!

📍 Your Situation

You have just arrived at Sydney Airport. You are getting into a taxi. Your destination is:

📍 42 George Street, Parramatta, NSW 2150

You have Australian cash and a card to pay.

Progress
1 of 8
🎯 Challenge 1 of 8
You get into the taxi. The driver looks at you in the mirror.
  • Greet the driver back
  • Give your full address
  • Include the suburb name
  • Use "Could you take me to..."
🇦🇺 Aussie Words:
"G'day" = "Good day" = Hello!
"Mate" = friendly word for anyone - man, woman, anyone!
"Heading" = going to / travelling to
💬 Sample Answer:

"G'day! Could you take me to 42 George Street, Parramatta please?"
🎯 Challenge 2 of 8
The driver starts chatting. He seems friendly and curious.
  • Where did you fly from?
  • Is this your first time in Sydney?
  • Keep it light and friendly!
🇦🇺 Aussie Words:
"No worries" = no problem / that's fine
"Yeah" = yes - very natural in Australia!
"Just landed" = just arrived by plane
💬 Sample Answer:

"Yeah, just landed from Vietnam! First time in Sydney actually. Feels amazing to be here!"

🇦🇺 Aussie note: "Yeah" is very natural in Australian casual speech!
🎯 Challenge 3 of 8
The driver wants to know why you moved to Australia.
  • What is your job here?
  • Sound excited and positive!
  • Keep it short and natural
🇦🇺 Aussie Words:
"Stoked" = very excited!
"Keen" = eager / looking forward to it
"Reckon" = think / believe
Example: "I reckon it'll be great!"
💬 Sample Answer:

"I'm here to work as a meat butcher! Got a job lined up in Parramatta. Pretty stoked about it!"

🇦🇺 Aussie note: "Stoked" = very excited!
🎯 Challenge 4 of 8
🚦 You are now on the highway. The meter is running.
You are not sure if the driver is going the right way.
  • Be polite but confident
  • Ask to confirm the route
  • Mention your destination again
🇦🇺 Aussie Words:
"Smooth sailing" = no problems / easy journey
"M4" = name of the motorway between Sydney and Parramatta
"Double check" = confirm / make sure
💬 Sample Answer:

"Sorry to bother you, but are we heading to Parramatta? Just want to double check!"
🎯 Challenge 5 of 8
You want to know how much the trip will cost.
  • Use "roughly" to ask politely
  • Ask if the toll is included
  • Don't be shy - it's normal to ask!
🇦🇺 Aussie Words:
"Toll" = money you pay to use the motorway/highway
"Fare" = the price of the taxi ride
"Roughly" = approximately / about
💬 Sample Answer:

"Excuse me, roughly how much will the fare be? Does that include the toll?"
🎯 Challenge 6 of 8
The driver asks how you want to pay.
  • Which do you prefer - cash or card?
  • Check if the machine works!
🇦🇺 Aussie Words:
"EFTPOS" = Electronic Funds Transfer - this is what Australians call the card payment machine!
"Love" = friendly term, like "mate" - don't be surprised to hear this!
"Card" = credit or debit card
💬 Sample Answer:

"Card thanks. Does your EFTPOS machine take Visa?"

🇦🇺 Aussie note: Australians say "EFTPOS" not "card machine"!
🎯 Challenge 7 of 8
🏠 You have arrived at 42 George Street, Parramatta!
The driver tells you the total amount. You need to pay.
  • What document do you need after every payment?
  • Be grateful and friendly!
🇦🇺 Aussie Words:
"Receipt" = printed proof of payment - always ask for this!
"Here you go" = natural way to hand something to someone
"Cheers" = thank you / goodbye in Australian English!
💬 Sample Answer:

"Here you go. Could I get a receipt please?"
🎯 Challenge 8 of 8
The driver wishes you good luck in Australia. Say goodbye properly!
  • Thank him warmly
  • Wish him something nice
  • Try using an Aussie expression!
🇦🇺 Aussie Words:
"Ripper" = excellent / fantastic!
"Arvo" = afternoon
"Cheers mate" = thanks friend - very common goodbye!
"Have a good one" = have a good day - very Australian!
💬 Sample Answer:

"Thank you so much, really appreciate it! You have a ripper day, mate!"

🇦🇺 Aussie note: "Ripper" = excellent / fantastic!
🎉 Well Done, Kim!

You completed all 5 taxi challenges!
