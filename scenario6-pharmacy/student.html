1) Start conversation

Your task: You just arrived in Australia and feel sick đau họng, sốt cao. Ask for help at the pharmacy counter.

💡 Show hint phrase

Say that you have a sore throat and a high fever, then ask the pharmacist to recommend suitable medicine.

💬 Show question hint

"Hi, I have a sore throat and a fever. Can you help me, please?"

2) Describe symptoms

Your task: Explain when the symptoms started and how you feel now mệt, đau đầu, ớn lạnh.

💡 Show hint phrase

Use a time phrase like since yesterday or for two days, then add extra symptoms such as tiredness, headache, or chills.

💬 Show question hint

"I have felt like this since yesterday, and I also have a headache and chills."

3) Add more details

Your task: Mention any other cold or flu symptoms ho, sổ mũi, nghẹt mũi.

💡 Show hint phrase

Add one or two more symptoms so the pharmacist has a clearer picture of your condition.

💬 Show question hint

"I also have a cough and a blocked nose."

4) Ask if you need a doctor

Your task: Ask whether you can buy medicine here or if you should see a doctor cần gặp bác sĩ không.

💡 Show hint phrase

Ask if your symptoms sound serious or if over-the-counter medicine is enough.

💬 Show question hint

"Do you think I need to see a doctor, or can I take medicine from here?"

5) Ask for medicine options

Your task: Ask for medicine không buồn ngủ that is suitable if you need to work or travel the next day.

💡 Show hint phrase

Ask for a non-drowsy option because you need to stay alert tomorrow.

💬 Show question hint

"Do you have anything non-drowsy? I need to work tomorrow."

6) Ask how to take it

Your task: Ask how to take the medicine properly trước/sau khi ăn.

💡 Show hint phrase

Check whether you should take it before food, after food, or with water.

💬 Show question hint

"Should I take it before or after food?"

7) Ask safety questions

Your task: Before buying, ask about dị ứng, tác dụng phụ and whether it is safe with your current medicine.

💡 Show hint phrase

Mention any allergies first, then ask about side effects and whether it is safe with what you are already taking.

💬 Show question hint

"Are there any side effects, and is it safe with the medicine I am taking now?"

8) Confirm dosage

Your task: Confirm the dosage clearly liều dùng.

💡 Show hint phrase

Repeat the dosage clearly to confirm how many tablets to take, how often to take them, and the daily maximum.

💬 Show question hint

"So how many tablets should I take, and what is the maximum in one day?"

9) Ask when to come back

Your task: Ask what to do if you do not feel better nếu không đỡ.

💡 Show hint phrase

Ask when you should come back or see a doctor if your symptoms get worse or do not improve.

💬 Show question hint

"If I do not feel better in a few days, should I come back or see a doctor?"

10) Payment and closing

Your task: Ask to pay by card, ask store closing time, and end politely thanh toán thẻ, giờ đóng cửa.

💡 Show hint phrase

Ask if you can pay by card, ask what time the store closes, and finish politely with thanks.
