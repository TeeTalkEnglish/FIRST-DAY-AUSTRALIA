Your Checklist

💳 What Type of Plan Can You Get?

In Australia, there are two main types of phone plans. As a new arrival, you can only get one of them. Read below to understand the difference.

✅

Prepaid

This Is For You!

💵 You pay first, then use the service
📅 Usually 28 or 30 days
🪪 No Australian ID needed
📝 No contract — cancel anytime
🛒 Buy at any shop or supermarket
🔄 "Recharge" means top up when it runs out
Example: You pay \$20 today → you get 15 GB for 28 days → after 28 days you recharge again or choose a different plan.
❌

Upfront (Postpaid)

You Cannot Get This Yet

💳 You use first, pay the bill later each month
📋 12 or 24 month contract
🪪 Needs Australian ID + credit check
🏦 Needs an Australian bank account
📱 Sometimes includes a free phone
⚠️ Early exit fee if you cancel
Example: You sign a 24-month contract → you get a new iPhone + 100 GB per month → you pay \$65/month bill → if you leave early you pay a penalty.
