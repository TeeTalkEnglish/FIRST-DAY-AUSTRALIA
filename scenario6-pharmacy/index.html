📖 Situation

Your student has a sore throat and mild fever on her first week in Australia. She visits a real-life style chemist and needs to listen carefully, explain symptoms, ask for medicine, understand instructions, and pay by card.

  • ✅ Listen first, then answer without a script
  • ✅ Use Australian pharmacy words (chemist, script, non-drowsy, EFTPOS)
  • ✅ Ask about medicine choice, safety, dosage, follow-up advice, and closing time

🏥 Real-life pharmacy examples

  • Chemist Warehouse (CBD branch example)
  • TerryWhite Chemmart (CBD branch example)

Tip: In Australia, opening hours vary by store. Check before you go.
