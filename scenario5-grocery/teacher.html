🎤 Challenge Mode — Teacher Script

In Hard (Challenge) mode, the student does NOT see what the store staff says. You read it aloud and the student types their response.

📋
How it works:
  1. The student's screen shows "🎤 Listen to your teacher"
  2. You read the green script below out loud
  3. The student types what they would say back
  4. The game scores their response for: correct item, amount, and politeness

⚙️ Game Configuration

When enabled, the student must listen to the teacher speak as the store staff, then choose their response. Focuses on listening and speaking skills.

You give spoken directions like left, right, between, behind, and opposite. The student must place each store section in the correct spot to unlock it.
