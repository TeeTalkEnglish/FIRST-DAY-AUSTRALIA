🔥 HARD MODE: Survival Simulation

Rules:
• Speak fast
• Use slang
• Do NOT repeat unless asked
• Student must understand meaning, not words

1. Notice

Say ONE of these (fast, natural):

  • “You gotta give me a week’s notice.”
  • “Can’t just leave like that, need a week heads up.”
  • “Nah, you gotta tell me earlier—like a week.”
Correct: Give one week notice

2. Bond

Say ONE:

  • “You’ll get it back in a couple days.”
  • “Takes a few days, I’ll send it through.”
  • “Yeah nah, bond comes back after a few days.”
Correct: Bond returned in a few days

3. Cleaning

Say ONE:

  • “Make sure it’s clean before you go.”
  • “Gotta clean the place up first.”
  • “Leave it tidy, yeah?”
Correct: Must clean before leaving

4. Inspection

Say ONE:

  • “I’ll swing by tomorrow.”
  • “I’ll come have a look tomorrow.”
  • “I’ll check it out tomorrow.”
Correct: Inspection tomorrow

5. Key

Say ONE:

  • “Just leave the key on the table.”
  • “Leave it inside.”
  • “Chuck the key on the table.”
Correct: Leave key on table
