🎮 How the Game Works

Complete each phase to finish your shopping trip

1

Get Your List

Receive a shopping list and a budget. Your goal is to find everything on the list without going over budget.

2

Enter the Store

Choose which section to visit first. Navigate between meat, produce, pantry, dairy, household, and drinks.

3

Shop & Talk

Add items to your cart and practice conversations with store staff. Choose the best response in each dialogue.

4

Checkout & Score

Pay for your items at regular checkout or try self-checkout. See your score, badges, and vocabulary learned!

🥩 Butcher Focus

Since you'll be working as a meat butcher, here's some extra vocabulary

🔪
At Work: As a butcher in Australia, you'll learn to break down carcasses, trim cuts, and serve customers at the counter. The vocabulary in this game will help you understand customer requests and read labels. You'll hear words like "mince", "rashers", "scotch fillet", and "chops" every day!
