🚖 In the Taxi

Navigate your taxi ride from the airport to your accommodation

📖 Situation

Kim Huynh has just arrived at Sydney Airport. She found the taxi rank and is now getting into a taxi. She needs to get to her accommodation at 42 George Street, Parramatta.

🎯 Your Goals

  • ✅ Greet the taxi driver naturally
  • ✅ Give your destination clearly
  • ✅ Ask about the fare and travel time
  • ✅ Make small talk during the ride
  • ✅ Pay correctly and say goodbye
