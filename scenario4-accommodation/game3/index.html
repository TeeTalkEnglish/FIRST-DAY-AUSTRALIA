🎮 Game 3 · House Rules Quiz

What Are the House Rules?

Dave explains the house rules — quiet time, cleaning roster, kitchen rules and rubbish days. Mai must listen carefully and answer questions correctly.

⚡ Medium 👥 2 Players ⏱ 12 min 📝 Listening Quiz
👩

Meet Mai Nguyen

Mai is a Vietnamese woman who has come to Australia to work as a meat butcher in regional Australia. She has just arrived at her new share house and needs to talk to her landlord Dave about the house rules.

🇻🇳 Vietnamese 🔪 Meat Worker 🇦🇺 Regional Australia 🏠 New Arrival
📖 How to Play
1
Teacher reads the rules

The teacher plays Dave the landlord. Read each house rule aloud — slowly and clearly. The student (Mai) listens only — no reading along!

2
Student answers the quiz

After all 4 rules, Dave asks 8 quiz questions. Mai chooses the correct answer from 4 options. Instant feedback is shown after each answer.

3
Review the results

At the end, both players see the score and a breakdown of every question. Review any rules Mai got wrong together.

🏠 Rules Covered in This Game
🌙
Quiet Time
10 pm weekdays · 9:30 pm weekends
🧹
Cleaning Roster
Bathroom Saturday · Shared areas Sunday
🍳
Kitchen Rules
Wash dishes straight away · No fish after 8 pm
🗑️
Rubbish Days
General: every Tuesday · Recycling: every 2nd Tuesday
📚 Key Vocabulary
quiet time
hours when you must be silent / quiet
cleaning roster
a schedule showing who cleans what
shared areas
rooms everyone in the house uses
label your food
write your name and date on your food
rubbish bin
the container for general waste / garbage
recycling bin
the container for bottles, cans, paper
bin lid
the cover / top of the rubbish bin
overnight
through the whole night / until morning
🎯 Learning Goals
  • Understand spoken instructions about house rules
  • Remember specific times, days and tasks after listening once
  • Use numbers and time expressions correctly (e.g. 10 pm, every two weeks)
  • Build vocabulary for shared accommodation in Australia
  • Practise active listening without reading support
