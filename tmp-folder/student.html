Lan – Vietnamese Meat-Plant Worker

Tomorrow you start your first shift cutting beef in Tamworth, NSW. You’ll call family on Messenger, so reliable data is the big priority.

📋

Your Checklist

Use this information while you talk to the shopkeeper.

  • Budget: Max \$25 for the first 28 days.
  • Data: Around 5 GB (you’ll use free Wi-Fi at the staff hostel).
  • Coverage: Must work in regional NSW (Telstra or Optus network preferred).
  • Calls: You’ll call home via Messenger/Zalo—no international minutes needed.
  • Group Option: Friend Huy arrives next week—ask if a “two-for” deal exists.

🗣️ Conversation Tasks

1
Greet the shopkeeper and say you’ve just flown in from Ho Chi Minh City.
2
Explain you mainly need data for Messenger, not international voice calls.
3
Ask to compare at least two real brands.
4
Negotiate: try to get a small discount or a free SIM for Huy.
5
Decide and confirm: “I’ll take the _____ plan, thank you.”

💬 Useful Phrases

“What’s the reception like in Tamworth?”
“Do I get free calls to Việt Nam, or international credit?”
“My friend arrives next week – any combo deal for two SIMs?”
“That’s a bit expensive. Can you do it for thirty-five?”
“Great, I’ll take that one. Can you activate it now?”
💡
Tip: If you forget a word, describe it: “the signal in the countryside” instead of “coverage.” Communication first – perfection later!
