🎮 What is this game?

This is Game 2 — Check-In Form Challenge.

The student (Mai) has a paper form with all her accommodation information. She must SPEAK this information to the landlord — she cannot show the form!

The teacher (Dave the Landlord) has a blank form and must type or write what Mai says. At the end, compare both forms to find mistakes!

⚡ Difficulty Level

Hard

This game is challenging because:
✴️ The form has tricky numbers and dates
✴️ Mai must spell her Vietnamese name, address and emergency contact
✴️ The student cannot show the form — speaking only!
✴️ Some information requires the student to ask questions back
✴️ The teacher will make deliberate mistakes to test listening!

How to Play

👩 Student (Mai) — Your Rules

  1. Open the Student Page — this is your paper form
  2. Do NOT show your form to the teacher!
  3. The teacher (Dave) will ask you questions
  4. Answer out loud in English using your form
  5. Spell difficult words when asked: "That's N-G-U-Y-E-N"
  6. If you don't understand a question, say: "Sorry, can you repeat that?"
  7. You have 15 minutes to complete the form!

👩‍🏫 Teacher (Dave the Landlord) — Your Rules

  1. Open the Teacher Page — this is your blank form to fill
  2. Ask each question from the script
  3. Make 3 deliberate mistakes — repeat information back incorrectly to test the student!
  4. Type what the student says into the form fields
  5. At the end, go to Results Page to check the score
  6. Award points based on the scoring guide
